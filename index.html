Our Project Scope

Game Identification

Identify all huntable game species present on the property.

Population Assessment

Assess which species are present in sufficient quantity to justify a hunting lease.

Harvest Feasibility

Evaluate the landscape dynamics to determine whether consistent and successful harvests are likely.

Wildlife Observation Report

Provide approximate counts of each huntable species observed on trail cams and via field spotting.

Habitat Improvement Suggestions

Offer practical suggestions for enhancing game presence (e.g., adding water sources, brush management, or tree clearing).
